# Product roadmap

## Data-connected release

- Replace demo repository state with typed Supabase queries and optimistic mutations.
- Generate database types in CI and require migration drift checks.
- Send transactional auth and support email through an approved provider.
- Add distributed rate limiting, structured logs, error monitoring, and audit exports.
- Connect private document upload/download with virus-scanning workflow.

## Planning depth

- Full guest address, plus-one, child, dietary, and household editing.
- Invitation waves, RSVP reminders, and communications history.
- Vendor payment schedules, linked tasks/documents, and comparison views.
- Collaborative comments, mentions, decision history, and partner notifications.
- Template personalization engine based on exact date and dependency rules.
- Full seating chart with tables, capacities, accessibility, and meal summaries.

## Padua operations

- Venue estimate, deposit, payment, enhancement, and menu-system integrations.
- Coordinator workload, handoff, response-time, and risk views.
- Versioned venue questions/resources with publish and rollback controls.
- Preferred-vendor onboarding, expiration, category ownership, and disclosure copy.
- Configurable tour scheduling connected to venue availability.

## Experience and intelligence

- Offline-friendly mobile capture for tours, notes, and inspiration.
- Calendar feeds and conflict-aware wedding-day scheduling.
- Permission-aware AI planning assistant grounded only in approved Padua resources.
- Color extraction and visual similarity for inspiration boards.
- Accessible floor-plan and seating tools.
- Spanish-language couple experience and localized date/currency support.

## Analytics and privacy

- Consent-aware adapter for PostHog, Plausible, GA, or Meta.
- Funnel, activation, completion, tour, and support dashboards.
- Data retention controls, account export/deletion, and legal hold workflows.
- Automated authorization, accessibility, performance, and dependency scanning.
